Travel Guide

  • Peak Season: June to September (warm weather, crowded)
  • Shoulder Season: April-May, October (mild weather, fewer crowds)
  • Off Season: November to March (cooler, many businesses closed)
  • Best Weather: May and September (perfect temperatures)
  • Oia Village - Famous blue-domed churches and sunset views
  • Fira Town - Capital with stunning caldera views
  • Red Beach - Unique red volcanic sand beach
  • Akrotiri Archaeological Site - Ancient Minoan city
  • Santo Wines Winery - Cliff-top wine tasting
  • Wear comfortable walking shoes for cobblestone streets
  • Book sunset dinner reservations well in advance
  • Rent an ATV or car to explore the island freely
  • Visit Oia early morning for photos without crowds
  • Carry cash as some small businesses don't accept cards
